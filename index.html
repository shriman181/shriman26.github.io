Feature Deep Dive

Built for the
Category Manager's Reality

🔴Real-Time OOS Detection

POS velocity models detect out-of-stock events within 30–60 minutes of occurrence. No cameras required. Alerts route directly to store manager and category manager simultaneously, with severity scoring based on revenue impact.

📋Planogram Compliance Scoring

Every store gets a weekly planogram compliance score by category. The system identifies which deviations are costing real revenue versus cosmetic issues, so category managers prioritize fixing what matters.

📱Mobile Audit App

Field reps photograph shelf sections; the AI model reads SKU placement, facing counts, and price tag accuracy in seconds. Data feeds back into the compliance model without manual entry.

🎯Assortment Gap Analysis

By clustering similar stores and analyzing market basket patterns, andromix identifies SKUs that should be stocked based on local demand but aren't — surfaced as revenue-opportunity alerts.

⚙️Markdown Trigger Engine

The system continuously compares projected sell-through against deadlines. When a gap is detected, it fires a markdown recommendation with exact depth, stores, and expected margin impact — calculated before you decide.

📈Demand Forecast at SKU-Store Level

LightGBM + LSTM ensemble model trained on 2+ years of POS history, incorporating weather, local events, seasonality, and price elasticity. Updated daily per store per SKU.

🏪Competitor Price Integration

Real-time competitor pricing feeds from Profitero, Wiser, and Edge by Ascential are incorporated into demand forecasts. When a competitor moves, andromix recalculates your markdown recommendation automatically.

🔁Post-Markdown Attribution Loop

Every markdown recommendation closes the loop by comparing predicted sell-through to actual. This feedback signal trains better recommendations over time — the model gets smarter on your specific store network.
